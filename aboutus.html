Apex Appraisal Services offers you in-depth experience.

Efficiency and professionalism are hallmarks of our service.

I’m Dan Pergolski, founder of Apex Appraisal Services, LLC. After working in the appraisal industry since 1996, I launched my own business in 2008 and specialize in serving clients in Rock and Green Counties. As your reliable, trusted and loyal partner, I commit to offering you these advantages:

  • Diligent, personalized service.
  • Faster turnaround time than most other companies.
  • Very competitive rates.

Underwriters appreciate my competence and willingness to go above and beyond in delivering quality service. Since I understand your sense of urgency, I routinely work weekends and evenings to meet deadlines.

Industry credentials

I’m passionate about real estate — which is why I learn as much as possible about the industry. I’ve developed in-depth expertise in several areas:

  • In 2004, I passed the Wisconsin State and National Exams for Certified Residential Appraiser.
  • In 2005, I was approved by HUD to grant FHA/FVA loans.
  • In 2007, I completed Rental Weatherization Inspector Certification — allowing me to inspect rental units to comply with weatherization codes.
  • In 2008, I became a licensed Wisconsin real estate salesperson.

I maintain my skills with continuing education classes — usually completing more courses than required for recertification.

A commitment to professionalism

To stay abreast of the latest industry developments and provide you with excellent service, I’m an active member of these professional organizations:

  • National Association of Realtors
  • Wisconsin Realtors Association
  • Century 21
  • Organization of Real Estate Professionals
